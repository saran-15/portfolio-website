Road Accident Analysis

The objective of the Road Accident Analysis project is to scrutinize daily-life road accidents, encompassing various vehicles such as cars, buses, bikes, and others. By examining accident data, the project aims to discern patterns, frequency, and severity of accidents involving different vehicle types. Through statistical analysis, it seeks to uncover trends regarding factors like location, time of day, weather conditions, and road type contributing to accidents. Ultimately, the project endeavors to offer actionable insights for enhancing road safety measures, informing policy decisions, and promoting public awareness campaigns to mitigate accidents and minimize their impact on communities.

Cricket Data Analytics

The project aims to leverage Power BI for comprehensive data analysis of Cricket World Cup statistics, with a specific focus on evaluating the performance of both bowlers and batsmen. By utilizing advanced analytics techniques, the objective is to identify top performers based on key metrics such as total wickets taken and runs scored, and subsequently assign appropriate valuations to these players. This analysis will provide valuable insights for team selection strategies and player pricing, ultimately enhancing decision-making processes in cricket management and fantasy leagues.
